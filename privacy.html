Privacy Policy

How we protect and respect your personal information

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

Koala Rescue Brisbane South Inc. ("we", "us", "our", or "our organisation") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website and when interacting with our organisation. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website, participate in our rescue operations, volunteer with us, or support our cause.

We comply with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) as set out in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and we are registered with the Australian Charities and Not-for-profits Commission (ACNC). We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about how we handle your personal information.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you have any questions about our privacy practices, don't hesitate to contact us using the information provided at the end of this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information in several ways, depending on how you interact with us. Here are the main types of information we may collect:

Koala Sighting and Rescue Reports

When you report a koala sighting or rescue situation, we collect information including the location of the koala, description of the animal's condition, your name and contact details, and any photographs or additional details you provide.

Volunteer Information

If you volunteer with us, we collect your name, contact details (phone number and email address), availability for rescue operations, and information about your skills and training. We may also keep records of your involvement with specific rescue operations and events.

Donor Information

When you make a donation to support our cause, we collect information about your donation through third-party platforms like GoFundMe. This may include your name, email address, and donation amount.

Website Usage Data

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring website. We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience.

Social Media Interactions

We engage with our community through Facebook and Instagram. If you interact with us on these platforms, we may collect information from those interactions. Please note that social media platforms have their own privacy policies that govern how they collect and use data.

How We Use Your Information

We collect and use your personal information for specific, legitimate purposes:

Rescue Operations

We use information from koala rescue reports to respond quickly and effectively to animals in distress. Your location details and contact information help us coordinate rescue efforts and follow up with you about the outcome.

Volunteer Coordination

We use volunteer information to organize rescue operations, coordinate training, schedule shifts, and communicate important updates about our activities.

Donation Processing

We use donor information to process your contribution, acknowledge your support, and keep you informed about the impact of your donation on our rescue and conservation work.

Communication

We may use your contact information to communicate with you about rescue updates, volunteer opportunities, upcoming events, and our organisation's activities. We only send communications that you have consented to receive.

Service Improvement

We use website analytics and feedback to improve our services, website functionality, and user experience.

Legal and Regulatory Compliance

We may use your information as required by Australian law, including compliance with our obligations as a registered charity with the ACNC and other relevant legislation.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Third-Party Services

We use several third-party services and platforms to support our operations. While we take reasonable steps to ensure these partners respect your privacy, they operate under their own privacy policies:

GoFundMe

We use GoFundMe to process donations. When you donate through GoFundMe, your transaction is governed by GoFundMe's own privacy policy.

Social Media Platforms

We maintain a presence on Facebook and Instagram for community engagement and updates. Your interactions with us on these platforms are subject to the privacy policies of those social media companies.

Website Analytics

Our website may use Google Analytics or similar analytics services to track website usage and gather insights about how visitors interact with our site.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Our Security Practices

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to safeguard your information, including:

  • Secure communication protocols for transmitting sensitive information
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular training for our team members on privacy and data protection
  • Appropriate physical and digital security measures for our systems

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Please note that while we implement security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your information to the best of our ability.

Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have several rights regarding your personal information:

Access to Your Information

You have the right to request access to personal information we hold about you. We will provide this information within a reasonable timeframe.

Correction of Information

If you believe that personal information we hold about you is inaccurate, incomplete, or out of date, you have the right to request that we correct it.

Deletion of Information

You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to any legal or operational requirements that necessitate retention.

Opt Out of Communications

You can opt out of receiving communications from us at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or by contacting us directly.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided in the "Contact Us" section of this policy.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you, remember your preferences, and understand how you use our website.

Types of Cookies We Use

We may use essential cookies (necessary for website functionality), analytics cookies (to understand how you use our site), and preference cookies (to remember your choices).

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 16 years of age. Our website is not directed to children, and we ask parents and guardians to supervise their children's online activities.

Volunteer Activities

If your child wishes to participate in our volunteer rescue activities, we may collect information from you (the parent or guardian) rather than directly from your child. We require parental or guardian consent before any person under 18 years of age participates in rescue operations or other activities.

If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 16 without proper parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Thank you for being part of our mission to protect Brisbane South's koalas. Your trust in us is precious, and we are committed to respecting and protecting your privacy.
