Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Introduction

Koala Rescue Brisbane South (KRBS) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or interact with our organisation.

Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Name, email address, and phone number (when you complete forms)
  • Information about your interest in volunteering or donating
  • Details of rescue incidents you report
  • Website usage data via analytics tools

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your volunteer enquiries and coordinate training
  • Process donations and provide receipts
  • Respond to koala rescue reports
  • Improve our website and services
  • Communicate about our mission and activities

Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Sharing Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with Queensland Department of Environment, Science and Innovation (for rescue coordination), veterinary partners (for animal care), and payment processors (for donation processing).

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us at 0424 807 470 to request changes.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at 0424 807 470 or via our volunteer contact form.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

KRBS

Koala Rescue Brisbane South is a community-driven rescue organisation. Mentored by John Knights with over 20 years of expertise, we respond 24/7 to injured koalas across Brisbane South.
