Why Volunteer With Us

Koala Rescue Brisbane South Inc. is a community-driven organisation that relies on passionate volunteers. We're always looking for people who want to make a real difference.

💪

Make a Real Difference

Your time directly saves koala lives. Every volunteer expands our reach and response time, ensuring more koalas get the help they need when they need it most.

🎓

Learn & Grow

Gain hands-on experience in wildlife rescue, conservation, and community education. Work alongside passionate volunteers and experts dedicated to koala welfare.

🤝

Join a Community

Connect with like-minded people who share your passion for protecting Australian wildlife. Be part of something meaningful and make lasting friendships.

OPPORTUNITIES

How You Can Help

🚑

Rescue Responder

Be on-call to assist with koala rescues. Training provided. Must be comfortable working outdoors and sometimes at heights. This is our most critical need.

🔍

Koala Spotter

Help monitor local koala populations by reporting sightings in your area. Perfect for residents of our service suburbs who can keep an eye out during daily activities.

📣

Community Educator

Help us spread the word about koala safety, road awareness, and what to do when you find a koala. Engage with local schools, events, and community groups.

🎉

Fundraising & Events

Help organise fundraising events, manage social media, or assist with community engagement activities. Your organisational skills help us reach more supporters.

Ready to Join?

The best way to get started is to reach out to us via Facebook Messenger. We'll chat about your interests, availability, and how you can best help our koalas. No experience necessary—we'll guide you through everything.

What to Expect

Flexible Commitment

We understand everyone has different availability. Whether you can help weekly, monthly, or just when you can spare a few hours, any time you can give helps our koalas.

Training Provided

New volunteers receive guidance and mentoring from experienced team members. We'll teach you koala handling, safety procedures, rescue techniques, and everything else you need to know.

Local Focus

You'll be helping koalas right in your own neighbourhood across Brisbane's southside. We serve Camp Hill, Mt Gravatt, Carina, Carindale, Holland Park, Coorparoo, Mansfield and surrounding suburbs.

QUESTIONS

Common Questions

Do I need experience with wildlife?

No! We welcome people from all backgrounds. Whether you're a complete beginner or have experience in animal care, we have a place for you. Training and mentoring are provided for all roles.

How much time do I need to commit?

It's flexible. Some volunteers help weekly, others respond when they can based on their personal schedule. Every bit counts. You set your own pace and we work around your availability.

What areas do you cover?

Brisbane's southside including Camp Hill, Mt Gravatt, Carina, Carindale, Holland Park, Coorparoo, Mansfield and surrounding suburbs. Peak rescue season runs August to February.

Is it safe?

Safety is our top priority. All rescue volunteers are trained in safe handling procedures and proper protocols. You'll never work alone on difficult rescues.

I can't volunteer but want to help?

You can donate to support our rescue efforts, share our social media posts to raise awareness, or simply report koala sightings to help us track local populations.

When is the best time to volunteer?

We always need volunteers, but peak season runs from August to February during koala breeding season when rescue calls increase. However, koalas need our help year-round!

Ready to Make a Difference?

Every volunteer brings us closer to our mission of protecting Brisbane South's koalas. Join our rescue team today.
