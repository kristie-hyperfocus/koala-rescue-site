YOUR IMPACT

Why Your Support Matters

For years, our dedicated team has operated completely self-funded, rescuing koalas out of their own pockets. Now as we formalise as a registered Australian charity, we need community support to expand our critical rescue operations.

🥅

Rescue Equipment

Specialised nets, carriers, climbing gear, and protective equipment for safe koala rescue operations in challenging urban environments.

🚗

Vehicle & Transport

Fuel, vehicle maintenance, and transport costs to reach koalas in distress 24/7 across Brisbane South when emergency calls come in.

🏥

Medical Supplies

First aid supplies, bandages, and medications for injured koalas before they reach wildlife hospitals, ensuring immediate care.
