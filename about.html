About Us

Meet the dedicated volunteers protecting koalas across Brisbane's southside

OUR STORY

How It All Started

Koala Rescue Brisbane South Inc. is a grassroots community organisation of dedicated volunteers committed to protecting and rescuing koalas across Brisbane's southside. Founded by John Knights—known as "Brisbane South's koala guy"—who holds the proper permits from the Department of Environment, Science and Innovation, our organisation started as a passion project funded from volunteers' own pockets.

For the past few years, we've been operating in a support capacity, responding to koala distress calls and working alongside expert partners like the Queensland Koala Society Inc. Now, we're formalising as an incorporated association and registered Australian charity to expand our reach and impact.

Our mission is clear: provide 24/7 emergency rescue response, coordinate rehabilitation care, educate our community, and actively monitor and protect koala populations across the Brisbane South suburbs we serve.

Peak rescue season runs from August to February during the koala breeding season, when we see an increase in displaced, injured, and orphaned koalas needing our help.

OUR MISSION

How We Protect Koalas

🆘

Rescue & Response

We provide 24/7 emergency response for sick, injured, orphaned, or displaced koalas. Our trained rescuers respond quickly to distress calls, ensuring every koala gets immediate help when they need it most.

📚

Community Education

Education is prevention. We raise awareness about road safety, habitat protection, and what to do when you find a koala in distress. An informed community is a koala's best defence.

🌿

Conservation

We monitor local koala populations and work to protect their urban habitat in Brisbane South. By understanding where koalas live and their needs, we can better advocate for their protection.

RECOGNITION

Our Impact & Achievements

🏆

2025 Community Grants People's Choice

We're proud to be a runner-up in the 2025 Community Grants People's Choice award. This recognition reflects the trust and support of the Brisbane South community who believe in our mission to protect koalas.

🚌

Peri the Bus Koala

In December 2025, our rescue team made headlines when Brianne Barkla was called to rescue Peri, a koala that made its way onto a Brisbane bus. The successful rescue went viral internationally.

✅

Registered Australian Charity

Now formally registered with the Australian Charities and Not-for-profits Commission (ACNC), we're committed to transparency, accountability, and maximising our impact on koala rescue and conservation.
