Your donation is tax deductible

Koala Rescue Brisbane South Inc. is a registered charity with the ACNC and holds DGR (Deductible Gift Recipient) status. This means we can issue a tax-deductible receipt for any donation of $2 or more. Keep your receipt at tax time: it all adds up.

Where the Money Goes

emergency

Emergency Response

Fuel, equipment, and on-call coordinators for 24/7 rescue operations across Brisbane South.

Training & Safety

Professional development for volunteers, safety gear, and ongoing education that keeps our team sharp.

Animal Care

Veterinary support, rehabilitation supplies, and temporary care for injured koalas on their road to recovery.

campaign

Community Outreach

Awareness campaigns, school visits, and signage that helps the community spot a koala in need before it's too late.
