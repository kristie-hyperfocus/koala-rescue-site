What to Expect

Volunteering with KRBS is rewarding, challenging, and sometimes intense. You'll work alongside experienced rescuers, learn professional techniques, and directly save koala lives across Brisbane South.

We're looking for people who are physically capable, mentally prepared for emergencies, focused on teamwork, and passionate about koalas. You don't need prior experience: just willingness to learn and commitment to the mission.

Every new volunteer is personally mentored by John Knights, with over 20 years of wildlife rescue experience. You'll gain skills that last a lifetime.

Volunteer Roles

There's a place for everyone: whether you're on the front line or behind the scenes.

emergency

First Responders

Respond to emergency calls, assess situations on the ground, and provide immediate care. Requires reliability and calm under pressure.

forest

Tree Climbers

Use professional climbing techniques to safely retrieve koalas from height. Requires training, fitness, and a head for heights.

groups

Rescue Support

Ground coordination, documentation, and equipment management. Essential to every operation: the backbone of every rescue.

timer

Trap Sitters

Monitor humane traps for injured animals. Requires patience, attention to detail, and the ability to sit quietly for extended periods.

directions_car

Transporters

Drive rescued animals to veterinary care or safe release locations. Requires your own vehicle and reliable availability.

campaign

Community Advocates

Spread awareness at events, manage social media, and organise fundraisers. Help people care about koalas before a crisis hits.

Training & Support

Every volunteer receives comprehensive training covering rescue protocols, animal handling, safety procedures, and wildlife science. We meet regularly for hands-on training and team development.

John Knights mentors new volunteers personally. You'll learn from someone who has handled thousands of rescues over two decades: and you'll be equipped with the skills and confidence to respond safely in any situation.

schedule Flexible commitment. We know life is busy. We work around your schedule and ask only that you show up when you say you will.
